Terms of Service

Welcome to implanted. By placing an order with us, you agree to the following terms. We keep things simple because we believe in trust and transparency.

1. Your Photos & Copyright

You promise that any photo you upload is yours or you have permission to use it. Please do not upload photos that you do not own the rights to (for example, photos taken by a professional photographer unless you have their permission).

2. Photo Requirements

To ensure your custom piece looks exactly like your best friend, the photo you upload MUST meet the following criteria:

  • Fully in Frame: Your pet's entire body (from ears to paws) must be clearly visible and mostly in the frame.
  • No Obstructions: Please avoid photos where grass, toys, cage bars, or other objects are blocking their face.
  • Good Lighting: We need nice, bright lighting to see their unique markings and features clearly. Shadows or blurriness make it hard for us to sculpt effectively.

3. Our Creation Process & Data Deletion

We use your uploaded photo as a reference to hand-sculpt a digital 3D model. This is an artistic interpretation, not a machine scan. Once your physical product is created and shipped, we permanently delete your original photo from our active production files. We do not keep a database of customer pet photos.

4. Returns on Custom Items

Because every plant stake is custom-made specifically for your pet, we cannot accept returns for "change of mind." However, if your item arrives broken or defective, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery, and we will make it right.

5. Product Care & Safety

Our plant stakes are 3D printed from PLA (a plant-based plastic). While durable, they can warp in extreme heat. Please do not put them in the dishwasher or leave them in a hot car.

Safety Warning: These are decorative items, not toys. They have pointed ends for inserting into soil. Please keep them out of reach of small children.
