Terms of Service

Listed below are implanted's terms of service. By placing an order with us, you agree to each and every one of them.

1. Copyright Laws

Your image must be owned by you, it cannot be the work of any other person, unless you have permission to use their photo. Obviously, using a photo of your pet that a relative took on their phone and sent to you is fine. Our main concern is with professional photography or copyrighted work, ensure you have the proper permissions to upload and use the photo.

2. Photo Requirements

To make our lives easier, you must have the following truths in your photo:

  • Fully in Frame: The part of your pet you want on the plant stake must be fully in frame, or else, we may have to ask for another photo.
  • Obstructed Viewing: Do not send in a photo of a pet with large objects obscuring significant amounts of their body. While a small ball, or blades of grass is fine, please do not make us infer on where parts of the body are.
  • Decent Lighting: Don't send a photo in awful lighting, or with bad resolution. We need to be able to see your pet's features and true colors to properly recreate them.
  • Minimal Accessories: If you let your pet wear a sweater or bandana, please ensure it does not have crazy colors or hard-to-recreate designs, as it makes our job much more difficult. While a cute bandana or collar is fine, consider taking off a tie dye or complicated set of clothes.
  • You can upload 1 photo for your order, if you use more than one, we will choose just one.

3. Creation and Deletion

The photo of your pet will stay on designated machines until the product has been created fully, at that point it will be deleted off of our servers and machines.

4. Custom Item Returns

Since every creation is different, we cannot accept returns for upset with how your product came out. However, we understand the shipping process is never perfect, and products do arrive broken at times. Please send us a message in the chat panel and we will send you a new product, free of charge.

5. Safety and Durability

The plant stake is 3D Printed out of a material named PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol-modified), better known for its outstanding material performance against UV, weathering, and other outside behaviors. However, it is not invincible, please keep it away from excess heat or functional stress. As well as this, extreme heat may cause warping. As it is plastic, it may be a good idea to keep it away from direct sunlight as much as possible.

Child Safety: These are slightly sharp stakes. Keep them out of reach from small children, as it may pose as a scratching risk.
